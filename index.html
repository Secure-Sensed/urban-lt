About Urban Living Technologies

Who We Are & What We Stand For

Urban Living Technologies (ULT) is a CAC-registered technology, cybersecurity, and digital transformation consulting firm headquartered in Oniru, Lagos, Nigeria. We deliver enterprise-grade technology solutions, cyber defense capabilities, and strategic advisory services to organisations across Africa.

Our mission is to empower African businesses with secure, innovative, and scalable solutions that support growth in the digital economy. We are committed to integrity, excellence, client success, and measurable impact.
